images:
	Images, surprisingly enough.  Non-transparent ones.  Automatically converted
	to data usable by the program by the makefile.

include:
	Include files!
	
psd:
	Source images, including Photoshop files.
	
source:
	Source code!
	
sprites:
	Sprite images.  They have a transparent background and are converted by the
	makefile in a different manner than the other images.
